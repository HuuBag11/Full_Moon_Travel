Ha Long Bay is the gift that nature gives to Vietnam with majestic mountains as beautiful as a mysterious and poetic painting. Many international tourism organizations have given a high rank for Ha Long Bay on the ranking chart of the most beautiful bay in the world.

Recently, the world-famous travel website -Touropia voted the top 10 most beautiful bays on the planet. Accordingly, Ha Long Bay was honored to take first place in the ranking. UNESCO also recognized Ha Long Bay as a world heritage site and has become one of the destinations which have been attracting millions of visitors each year.

You Want to see Halong Bay but don’t have much time? No problem. Join us on a one-day tour for an amazing adventure you won’t forget, Perfect for busy travelers and nature lovers.

Ha Long Bay Luxury Day Boat

Ha Noi Departure 8.00 : Sao Viet Office ( 114 Tran Nhat Duat Street – Ha Noi Old Quater )

Ninh Binh Departure 7.15 : Ninh Binh Footprint Travel – Tam Coc, Ninh Binh

Tuan Chau island Departure 11.20 : The Pine Tree Coffee & Restaurant – Tuan Chau Port

Cat Ba Departure 7.40 : 213 Mot Thang Tu Steet, Cat Ba Town ( Full Moon Party Tour office )

Ha Long Bay Day Tour itinerary:

– 8h10 – 8h50: English speaking guide will pick you up at your hotel – around Ha Noi Old Quarter area. ( Optional: Cat Ba island Departure: 7h40, Ninh Binh Departure: 7h15)

– 9:00 – 11:30: Start two and a haft hours road journey to Quang Ninh Province via express way. Quick stop over for stretching your legs on the way.

– 11:45 – 12:15: Arrive to Ha Long Bay – Embark from Tuan Chau Marina onto Cruise’s Office. A complimentary welcome drink will be served.

Ha Long Bay Day Boat

The boat starts cruising.

–  12:30: Enjoy Buffet lunch with Vietnamese traditional food while cruising though thousands of limestone islands such as: Dog Rock, Insence Bunner and especially Fighting Cock – a symbol of Ha Long Bay.

Cruise Buffet Lunch

– 13:30: Explore Sung Sot Cave – The longest, biggest and the most beautiful grotto of Ha Long Bay.

Surprising Cave Ha Long Bay

– 14:45: The boat will move to Luon Cave area – a water cave for kayaking or rowing bamboo boat or just stay on board for relaxing.

Kayaking Or Bamboo Boat i Luon Cave

–  15:45: Visiting Titop island for swimming or hiking to the Titop peak for taking photos with panoramic view of the bay.

TiTop island Ha Long Bay

– 16:30: It is time for a rest after activities and capture the sunset on the bay. Complimentary Vietnamese tea, fresh juice, fruit, ang little cake will be served. Enjoy the return sail back.

Sunset party on Deck

– 17:45 – 18:00: Back to Ha Long harbor. Disembark to get on the bus, moving back Hanoi.

Limousine Bus

– 20:30 – 21:00: Our guide will drop you off at your hotel (around the Old Quarter area). Thank you so much for choosing Full Moon Travel.
